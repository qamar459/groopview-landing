Groopview Inc. Launches SWIS: AI-Powered Remote Assistance Revolutionizes Customer Care and Field Operations

Philadelphia, PA - Groopview Inc., a pioneer in intelligent live remote communication, announces the official release of SWIS (See What I See) for Customer Care and Field Operations, transforming remote assistance with dual-camera technology and AI-powered insights.

SWIS enhances customer service, field support, and remote diagnostics through key features including:

  • Multi-Way Video Calls for real-time collaboration

  • Dual-Camera Streaming for a true "See What I See" experience

  • AI-Powered Call Summaries with sentiment analysis

  • Snapshot & Annotation Tools for improved troubleshooting

  • Call Recording & Analysis for quality control and training

  • Client Portal & Analytics with real-time dashboards

Delmond Newton, CEO of Groopview Inc. , states, "SWIS was designed to eliminate inefficiencies in customer support and field operations by enabling live remote assistance. With AI-powered capabilities and dual-camera functionality, SWIS enhances customer interactions, optimizes workforce productivity and reduces costs."

Tech Network Inc., an early adopter of SWIS, reported significant improvements:

  • 67.3% reduction in repeat jobs

  • 100% vehicle inspection compliance achieved in 6 days (previously 20+ days)

  • 40% reduction in operational expenses

  • 390 fewer 100-mile trips annually, saving ~1,700 gallons of fuel

Aaron Thornburgh, Chief Financial Officer at Tech Network Inc., comments, "SWIS transformed how we manage our field teams. Real-time coaching, job-site verification, and reduced travel have improved efficiency, reduced costs, and enhanced job quality. We're excited to expand SWIS to other regions."
